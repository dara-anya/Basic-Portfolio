About Me

Dara Anya graduated from the University of Texas at Austin in 2016. He graduated with a Bachelors of Science in Neuroscience with a focus in psychology. Following his graduation, Dara started pursuing his passion for filmmaking. He made his directorial and actorial debut in his narrative short film: Forced To Swim. His short was nominated for ‘Best Experimental/Art-house/Silent Short’ and ‘Best Original Music’ in the Southern States Indie FanFilmFest 2017 selection. Since, Dara has created an experimental short film: Natural Beauty.
